What We Do

LinkedIn + Substack, done for you.

Built from your thinking, delivered weekly.

FoundersFlow helps founders, CEOs, and senior leaders build a credible personal brand on LinkedIn and Substack, without spending hours writing. We turn your thinking, experience, and perspective into consistent written content that builds trust over time.

Our Approach

Human-led, AI-optimised

01

Human-crafted

Every piece of content is reviewed, refined, and finalised by a human.

Judgment, nuance, tone, and restraint come first.

02

AI-optimised

We use AI to extract patterns and support efficiency:

  • language patterns
  • phrasing habits
  • structure and optimisation

AI supports the process, it does not replace thinking or authorship.

03

Seamless flow

Our goal is to remove friction from writing.

Clients don’t need to “sit down and write” or manage complex workflows. Content happens in the background while they focus on building.

How It Works

A simple weekly rhythm

01

Step 1 — Onboarding & Direction

We start with a call to understand:

  • how the client thinks
  • how they make decisions
  • what they want to be known for

This is about context, not content ideas.

02

Step 2 — Voice & Pattern Capture

We collect existing material such as:

  • emails
  • texts
  • LinkedIn posts
  • Substack drafts
  • WhatsApp voice notes

We analyse patterns in how they communicate and build a voice profile.

03

Step 3 — Ongoing Input

Clients can send:

  • weekly thoughts
  • trends
  • topics
  • quick voice notes

This keeps content current without creating pressure.

04

Step 4 — Weekly Delivery

We draft your content in Google Docs, organised by week. You review, comment, and approve posts before anything goes live, giving you full visibility and control over what’s published. We send written posts weekly (typically Monday morning), ready to post or we offer a scheduling service meaning the posts roll out whilst you build.
